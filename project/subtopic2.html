Url Future Job Loss of AI
Site Author Harvard Business School
Description AI is extremely inventive and keeps learning more and more daily with the inputs it is given. Because AI is learning so quickly it is becoming a staple of the world of coding and business. It is already starting to take over certain jobs and instead of eliminating the job, it will teach itself how to do it and then in turn lowering the job market for that job. Certain jobs are more at risk than others. Some jobs are coding, software engineers, and other jobs that are easy for an AI bot to learn. Some jobs protected are teachers, doctors, and other medical professions that require years of research and knowledge.
